Third Declension—continued.

Nouns ending in e like 'mare,' sea, of the 3rd Declension are Neuter. They form the Genitive Plural in ium, the Nominative and Accusative Plural in ia, and the Ablative Singular (like the Dative Singular) in ī. Thus

SINGULAR. PLURAL.
1, 2, 3 mare maria
4 maris marium
5 marī maribus
6 in marī in maribus
