Third Declension—continued.
NOUNS LIKE 'FLUMEN,' 'OPUS,' 'TEMPUS.'

Neuters ending in men have stems ending in min- from which the other cases are formed. Neuters ending in us have stems in er- or or-. The last vowel of these stems is always short.

Note that the Neuters have the Accusative the same as the Nominative, both in the Singular and in the Plural Number (as in 2nd Declension), and that their Nominative and Accusative Plural end in a (also as in the 2nd Declension).
