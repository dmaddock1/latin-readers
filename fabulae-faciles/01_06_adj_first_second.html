7. Adjectives of First and Second Declension. (See Note 6.)

Lat. Prim., §32.

Like Bonus.

  • Magn-ŭs, -ă, -um, great.
  • Mult-ŭs, -ă, -um, many.
  • Me-ŭs, -ă, -um, my.
  • Tu-ŭs, -ă, -um, thy, your.
  • Su-ŭs, -ă, -um, his, her, its, their.

Like Niger.

  • Pul-chĕr, -chră, -chrum, beautiful.
  • Nos-tĕr, -tră, -trum, our.
  • Ves-tĕr, -tră, -trum, your.
